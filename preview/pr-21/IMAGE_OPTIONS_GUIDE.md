## How to Use These Images

### For Icons:
1. Download your chosen image
2. Save it as: `images/icon.png` or `images/icon.svg`
3. Ensure it's square (500x500px minimum recommended)

### For Backgrounds:
1. Download your chosen image
2. Save it as: `images/background.jpg`
3. Recommended size: 1920x1080px or larger

### All Stock Photo Sites Listed:
- **Unsplash** - Free, no attribution required
- **Pexels** - Free, no attribution required
- Both allow commercial use
