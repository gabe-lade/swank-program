Hilliard Housing Affordability Tracker

Tracking Hilliard's home prices, rents, and housing production against peer communities in Franklin County, Ohio.

Author: Gabriel E. Lade.

From 2025 to 2026, I volunteered on the Hilliard, Ohio housing steering committee to review the city's housing study. One goal of the study was to track whether Hilliard has met its goal of remaining a place that meets the needs of its current and future residents. I developed this tool to help the city track its housing prices and production relative to other comparison cities in Franklin County, Ohio.

Draft. All panels are computed from real data except the new-housing-permits panel, which is an illustrative placeholder pending City of Hilliard Building Division data.
