C. William Swank Program in Rural-Urban Policy

The Ohio State University

The C. William Swank Program in Rural-Urban Policy at The Ohio State University supports research and outreach on the economic and policy connections between rural and urban communities.

The program brings together researchers, policymakers, and stakeholders to address critical questions about energy, environment, agriculture, and economic development across the rural-urban landscape.

Research

Rigorous economic research on energy, environmental, and agricultural policy with an emphasis on rural-urban connections.
