The C. William Swank Program in Rural-Urban Policy at The Ohio State University conducts research and outreach on the economic and policy issues connecting rural and urban communities in Ohio.

The program brings together researchers, extension educators, and stakeholders to address critical questions about land use, agriculture, energy, environment, and economic development across the rural-urban landscape.
