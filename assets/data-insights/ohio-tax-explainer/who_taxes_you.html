About this map

A parcel can fall inside both a city and a township. Whether the township actually taxes you depends on your specific taxing district — some city residents pay their township (e.g. Hilliard/Norwich), others pay none (e.g. Toledo). This map names general-purpose governments only; special districts — library, parks, JVSD/vocational, zoo, port, ambulance, developmental-disabilities — also tax the parcel but aren’t drawn here.
