Where lost ag land went

Agricultural land is NLCD 81 Pasture/Hay plus 82 Cultivated Crops. Area metrics are counted from the original 30 m Albers Equal Area NLCD pixels; the web map is displayed in EPSG:3857. Gross loss is begin-year agricultural land that is non-agricultural by the end year. Gross gain is begin-year non-agricultural land that is agricultural by the end year. Net loss equals gross loss minus gross gain. Share of loss to development equals Lost to development (acres) divided by Gross loss (acres). Net loss rate is net loss divided by Agricultural land, begin year (acres).
